About

Where Energy Meets Quantitative Finance

Anemos Energy Trading LLC is a systematic investment manager focused on wholesale electricity markets in the United States. We operate exclusively through financial instruments, with no ownership of physical energy infrastructure.


Electricity markets are governed by physical constraints, weather, and grid topology — factors entirely distinct from the macroeconomic drivers of traditional asset classes. This structural independence creates a differentiated source of return for sophisticated quantitative participants.

Our Approach

Systematic. Data-Driven.
Disciplined.

Our process integrates diverse data sources with advanced quantitative methods to model market dynamics and identify opportunities. A systematic optimization layer translates these insights into trading decisions designed to generate consistent, risk-adjusted returns.

⚡

Wholesale Power Markets

We focus on financial trading within the structured two-settlement system of U.S. electricity markets, operating purely through virtual financial instruments with no physical asset requirements.

🤖

Quantitative Models

Advanced machine learning and statistical models continuously adapt to evolving market conditions, informing a disciplined and repeatable investment process.

📊

Portfolio Diversification

Electricity market returns are driven by localized physical and meteorological factors, offering a source of alpha that is structurally uncorrelated with equities, bonds, and other traditional asset classes.

🏦

Capital Efficiency

The strategy is designed to be highly capital-efficient. A portion of fund assets is held in secure, liquid instruments while satisfying market operator collateral requirements.

🌐

Scalable Infrastructure

Our technology and research infrastructure is built to operate across multiple power grids and market structures, supporting disciplined geographic expansion over time.

🛡️

Risk Management

Positions are distributed across locations and time intervals, with systematic controls designed to limit exposure to any single market event or idiosyncratic outcome.

The Opportunity
As global electricity demand accelerates, wholesale power markets present a growing opportunity for disciplined systematic participants.
